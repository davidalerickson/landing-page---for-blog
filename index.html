    bio

    Design and Technology are my Passion

    I have 30 years of software and technology experience. I led a team of support engineers at Microsoft from 1989 to 1994. I cofounded Wirefrog, a freelanceing business focusing on web site design and multimedia. Starting in 2004 I moved to Brazil and became a coconut farmer. Upon returning to the United States in 2014 I decided to study modern web developement and have taken bootcamp style courses as well as courses specializing in React, Gatsby, Svelte and other frameworks. I am also skilled in digital media preparation and production.
